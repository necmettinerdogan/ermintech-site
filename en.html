About Us

EMT Erdogan Mining Technologies is an engineering company providing engineering, project development and commercial solutions in the fields of mining, mineral processing, and mineral process technologies. Our company develops industry-specific solutions by combining technical knowledge, field experience, and a process-oriented approach on various mineral groups, especially industrial minerals such as magnesite, dolomite, bentonite, and quartz sand.


Our activities include crushing-sieving, grinding, classification, drying, microwave-assisted thermal processes, mineral beneficiation, and process optimization. In particular, our studies on energy-efficient microwave drying and thermal processing technologies aim to develop more controlled, sustainable, and innovative applications in mineral processing systems.


Our company not only provides engineering and project development services but also supplies equipment, machinery, and consumables for the mining and mineral industries. We act as a solution partner in many industrial product groups such as grinding balls, grinding rods, casting sand additives, bentonite products, and process equipment.


Erdoğan Mining Technologies combines field experience, academic background, and applied engineering to approach every project with technical accuracy, economic feasibility, and long-term efficiency perspective. Our goal is to create reliable, innovative, and sustainable solutions in mining and mineral technologies, adding value to the industry.
