About Us

EMT Erdoğan Mining Technologies Company;

We are an engineering and consulting company providing engineering, project development, and commercial solutions in the fields of mining operations, ore beneficiation, and mineral processing technologies.


Our Fields of Activity;

  • Mining engineering services
  • Crushing, screening, grinding, and classification systems
  • Mineral beneficiation (magnetic separation, electrostatic separation, leaching, etc.)
  • Dewatering and drying processes
  • Utilization of mining plant tailings
  • Microwave-assisted thermal processing technologies
  • Process design and optimization

In particular, our work on energy-efficient microwave drying and thermal processing technologies aims to develop more controlled, sustainable, and innovative applications in mineral processing operations.


Mineral Recovery;

We carry out studies focused on the recovery of valuable minerals from beneficiation plant tailings using methods such as flotation, shaking tables, jigs, and dense media separation. The remaining clay and fine fractions after separation are further evaluated and converted into secondary raw materials for the ceramics and refractory industries.


Mining Equipment Supply;

We also supply equipment, machinery, and consumables for the mining industry. We act as a reliable solution partner in various industrial product groups such as grinding media, grinding rods, and process equipment.


Industrial Minerals;

We develop application-specific solutions by combining technical expertise and field experience in various mineral groups, especially magnesite, dolomite, bentonite, and quartz sand, with process engineering.


Our Approach;

We approach every project by combining field experience, academic knowledge, and applied engineering, with a focus on technical accuracy, economic feasibility, and sustainability.


Our Objective;

Our goal is to create innovative, reliable, and applicable solutions in the fields of mining and mineral technologies, thereby adding value to the industry.
